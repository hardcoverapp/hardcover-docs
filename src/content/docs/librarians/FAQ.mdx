## Privileges

### What privileges do librarians get?
Librarians can edit most fields of books, editions and authors. In the future, librarians will also be able to edit series, characters and other book related data.

### What privileges do supporters get?
Supporters get the same permissions as librarians as long as they remain supporters.

### What‚Äôs to stop a librarian from messing up a book/author/etc?
For books/editions with a high 'read score' - generally anything with 5 or more reads - there is a check in place that sends significant changes, such as merges or deletes, to the head librarian's queue for approval before the change is applied. That said, librarians have a lot of power - if you're ever unsure about an action you're taking, please ask first!

### If you‚Äôre an approved librarian, can you also become a supporter?
Yes, please do - Hardcover would love to have your support! Supporters retain the full edit permisions of a librarian. Librarians get many of the benefits that supporters get for free, including edit ability across the site.
